# Sample Walkthrough Questions

## Purpose

This document provides sample walkthrough questions that may be used during a GRC assessment, internal audit, SOC 2 readiness review, PCI readiness review, or NIST-based assessment.

The purpose is to demonstrate how a GRC analyst can interview control owners, understand processes, validate evidence, and identify control gaps.

## How to Use These Questions

These questions may be used during meetings with:

- Control owners
- System owners
- Security operations teams
- IAM teams
- Infrastructure teams
- Vendor risk teams
- GRC teams
- Internal audit teams
- Business owners

## Governance and Risk Management

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| How are cybersecurity policies reviewed and approved? | Confirms policy governance and management oversight. |
| Who owns the enterprise risk register? | Confirms risk ownership and accountability. |
| How often are cybersecurity risks reviewed by management? | Confirms risk review cadence. |
| How are high-risk findings escalated? | Confirms escalation process. |
| How is remediation progress reported to leadership? | Confirms executive visibility. |

## Identity and Access Management

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| How are new users approved before access is granted? | Confirms access approval process. |
| How are users removed when they leave the company? | Confirms termination process. |
| How often are access reviews performed? | Confirms review frequency. |
| Who approves access to critical systems? | Confirms system owner accountability. |
| How are access review results documented? | Confirms evidence quality. |

## Privileged Access Management

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| How are privileged accounts approved? | Confirms administrative access governance. |
| Are privileged accounts protected by MFA? | Confirms strong authentication. |
| How often are privileged accounts reviewed? | Confirms periodic oversight. |
| Are shared administrator accounts allowed? | Identifies shared account risk. |
| How are privileged access exceptions documented? | Confirms exception management. |

## Vulnerability Management

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| How often are vulnerability scans performed? | Confirms scan frequency. |
| How are critical vulnerabilities prioritized? | Confirms risk-based remediation. |
| What are the remediation timelines for critical and high vulnerabilities? | Confirms SLA expectations. |
| How are overdue vulnerabilities escalated? | Confirms management oversight. |
| How is remediation validated? | Confirms closure evidence. |

## Logging and Monitoring

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Which systems send logs to the SIEM? | Confirms log source coverage. |
| How is the log source inventory maintained? | Confirms ownership and completeness. |
| Who reviews security alerts? | Confirms monitoring responsibility. |
| How are alerts escalated? | Confirms incident escalation. |
| How long are logs retained? | Confirms retention expectations. |

## Incident Response

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Where is the incident response plan maintained? | Confirms documentation location. |
| Who is responsible for leading an incident response? | Confirms roles and responsibilities. |
| How are incidents classified by severity? | Confirms severity model. |
| When was the last tabletop exercise performed? | Confirms response testing. |
| How are lessons learned tracked? | Confirms continuous improvement. |

## Vendor Risk Management

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| How are vendors risk-ranked before onboarding? | Confirms vendor risk tiering. |
| What evidence is requested from high-risk vendors? | Confirms due diligence process. |
| How often are high-risk vendors reviewed? | Confirms ongoing monitoring. |
| How is vendor access reviewed? | Confirms third-party access governance. |
| How are vendor risks tracked? | Confirms remediation and ownership. |

## Data Protection

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| How is sensitive data classified? | Confirms data classification process. |
| Where is sensitive data stored? | Confirms data inventory and storage awareness. |
| Is sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit? | Confirms data protection controls. |
| Who can access restricted data? | Confirms least privilege. |
| How is sensitive data shared with vendors? | Confirms third-party data protection. |

## Backup and Recovery

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Which systems are backed up? | Confirms backup scope. |
| How often are backups performed? | Confirms backup frequency. |
| How are backup failures reviewed? | Confirms monitoring process. |
| When was the last restoration test completed? | Confirms recovery validation. |
| How are restoration test results documented? | Confirms evidence quality. |

## PCI Readiness

| Question | Purpose |
|---|---|
| What systems are included in the cardholder data environment? | Confirms PCI scope. |
| How is the cardholder data environment segmented? | Confirms network separation. |
| When was segmentation last tested? | Confirms validation evidence. |
| Who has access to payment systems? | Confirms access governance. |
| Are payment environment logs reviewed? | Confirms monitoring expectations. |

## Summary

Walkthrough questions help the assessment team understand how controls operate in practice.

Strong walkthroughs help identify evidence gaps, process weaknesses, unclear ownership, and remediation needs.
