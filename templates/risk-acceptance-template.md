# Risk Acceptance Template

## Purpose

This template is used to document formal management acceptance of cybersecurity, compliance, operational, or third-party risk.

Risk acceptance should be approved, time-bound, documented, and reviewed before expiration.

## Risk Description

Describe the risk being accepted.

Include:

- What control gap exists
- What system or process is affected
- What threat or business issue creates the risk
- What could happen if the risk occurs

## Business Justification

Explain why the risk is being accepted instead of remediated immediately.

Possible reasons include:

- Technical limitation
- Vendor dependency
- Budget limitation
- Operational constraint
- Business continuity concern
- Pending replacement system
- Project dependency
