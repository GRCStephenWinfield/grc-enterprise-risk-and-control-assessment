# Company Profile

## Company Name

BayouTrust Digital Bank

## Industry

Banking, Fintech, and Technology

## Company Overview

BayouTrust Digital Bank is a fictional regional digital bank and fintech company that provides online banking, digital payment services, ACH processing, business banking, and customer account management.

The company operates a cloud-hosted banking platform, supports a remote workforce, uses third-party vendors, and processes sensitive customer and payment information.

## Company Size

- 1,200 employees
- 500,000 customers
- 3 primary business units: Retail Banking, Digital Payments, and Business Banking
- Remote and hybrid workforce
- Cloud-hosted production environment

## Sensitive Data

The company stores, processes, or transmits the following data types:

- Customer personally identifiable information
- Financial records
- Payment card data
- Authentication data
- Vendor records
- Employee records
- Internal business information

## Technology Environment

The company uses:

- Cloud infrastructure
- Online banking application
- Mobile banking application
- Payment processing platform
- Customer relationship management system
- Identity and access management platform
- Security information and event management system
- Vulnerability scanning tools
- Help desk ticketing system
- Third-party service providers

## Regulatory and Compliance Drivers

BayouTrust Digital Bank must consider the following cybersecurity and compliance expectations:

- NIST Cybersecurity Framework
- NIST SP 800-53
- CIS Controls
- SOC 2
- PCI DSS
- Vendor risk management
- Internal audit expectations
- Customer data protection requirements

## Assessment Purpose

The purpose of this assessment is to evaluate BayouTrust Digital Bank’s cybersecurity governance, risk management, and control environment. The assessment identifies control gaps, documents enterprise risks, recommends remediation actions, and prepares executive-level reporting.
