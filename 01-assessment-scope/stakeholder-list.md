# Stakeholder List

## Purpose

This document identifies the fictional stakeholders involved in the BayouTrust Digital Bank GRC assessment.

The purpose is to show how assessment responsibilities, evidence ownership, remediation ownership, and leadership reporting can be assigned across an organization.

## Stakeholder Summary

A successful GRC assessment requires coordination across security, IT, compliance, risk, business, vendor management, and executive leadership teams.

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder Role | Department | Assessment Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| Chief Information Security Officer | Executive Leadership | Provides executive sponsorship and reviews high-risk findings. |
| Chief Information Officer | Executive Leadership | Supports remediation planning and technology risk decisions. |
| GRC Manager | Governance, Risk, and Compliance | Leads assessment coordination, evidence tracking, risk register, and POA&M. |
| IAM Manager | Identity and Access Management | Provides access control, MFA, and privileged account evidence. |
| Security Operations Manager | Security Operations | Provides SIEM, incident response, and vulnerability management evidence. |
| Infrastructure Manager | IT Operations | Provides patching, backup, server, and remediation evidence. |
| Cloud Security Manager | Cloud Security | Provides cloud asset, logging, access, and encryption evidence. |
| Network Security Manager | Network Security | Provides firewall, segmentation, and network security evidence. |
| Vendor Risk Manager | Vendor Management | Provides vendor assessments, vendor access reviews, and third-party risk evidence. |
| Application Owners | Business and Technology Teams | Provide application access review and control operation evidence. |
| Internal Audit Lead | Internal Audit | Reviews assessment results and audit readiness. |
| Legal Counsel | Legal | Advises on legal, contractual, and regulatory considerations. |
| Business Unit Leaders | Business Operations | Review business impact and support remediation decisions. |
| Executive Leadership Team | Senior Leadership | Reviews risk posture, accepts risk when appropriate, and approves resources. |

## Stakeholder Responsibilities

### GRC Team

The GRC team is responsible for:

- Coordinating the assessment
- Maintaining the evidence request tracker
- Reviewing evidence
- Documenting findings
- Maintaining the risk register
- Building the POA&M
- Preparing executive reports

### Security Operations Team

The Security Operations team is responsible for:

- Providing SIEM evidence
- Providing vulnerability scan reports
- Providing incident response documentation
- Supporting logging and monitoring remediation
- Supporting tabletop exercise planning

### IAM Team

The IAM team is responsible for:

- Providing MFA reports
- Providing privileged account lists
- Supporting access review activities
- Remediating privileged access gaps
- Supporting access control evidence requests

### Infrastructure Team

The Infrastructure team is responsible for:

- Providing patch management evidence
- Providing backup reports
- Performing restoration testing
- Supporting vulnerability remediation
- Providing system ownership information

### Vendor Risk Team

The Vendor Risk team is responsible for:

- Providing vendor inventory
- Providing vendor due diligence evidence
- Coordinating vendor access reviews
- Tracking vendor-related findings
- Supporting third-party risk reporting

### Executive Leadership

Executive leadership is responsible for:

- Reviewing top risks
- Approving resources
- Reviewing overdue remediation items
- Accepting residual risk when appropriate
- Supporting accountability for remediation

## Communication Cadence

| Meeting or Report | Audience | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Assessment Status Meeting | GRC team and control owners | Weekly | Review evidence requests, blockers, and findings. |
| POA&M Review | GRC team and remediation owners | Monthly | Track remediation progress. |
| Executive Risk Update | Senior leadership | Monthly | Report top risks and remediation status. |
| Audit Readiness Review | GRC, internal audit, control owners | Monthly or quarterly | Review evidence and audit readiness. |

## Summary

This stakeholder list shows how a GRC assessment requires coordination across many business and technology groups.

Clear ownership helps ensure that evidence is collected, findings are remediated, and risk is reported to leadership.
