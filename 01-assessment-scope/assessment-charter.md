# Assessment Charter

## Assessment Name

Enterprise Risk and Control Assessment

## Assessment Objective

The objective of this assessment is to evaluate the organization's cybersecurity control environment, identify gaps, document risks, and recommend remediation activities.

This assessment is designed to demonstrate practical Governance, Risk, and Compliance skills across multiple cybersecurity and compliance frameworks.

## Assessment Scope

The assessment covers governance, risk management, identity and access management, vulnerability management, logging and monitoring, incident response, vendor risk, data protection, PCI readiness, SOC 2 readiness, and audit readiness.

## In-Scope Areas

The following areas are included in the assessment:

- Governance and risk management
- Identity and access management
- Privileged access management
- Asset management
- Vulnerability management
- Patch management
- Logging and monitoring
- Incident response
- Vendor risk management
- Data protection
- Backup and recovery
- PCI DSS readiness
- SOC 2 readiness
- Executive risk reporting

## Out-of-Scope Areas

The following areas are excluded from the assessment:

- Physical security testing
- Penetration testing
- Source code review
- Financial statement audit
- Legal review
- Production system changes
- Employee background investigations

## Frameworks Used

This assessment uses the following frameworks and standards:

- NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0
- NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5
- CIS Controls v8.1
- SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria
- PCI DSS v4.0.1
- POA&M methodology

## Assessment Activities

The assessment includes the following activities:

- Reviewing policies and procedures
- Mapping controls to multiple frameworks
- Requesting and reviewing evidence
- Assessing control design effectiveness
- Assessing control operating effectiveness
- Documenting findings
- Creating a risk register
- Performing a gap analysis
- Developing a POA&M
- Preparing executive-level reporting

## Expected Deliverables

The expected deliverables include:

- Control assessment summary
- Evidence request list
- Risk register
- Gap analysis report
- POA&M
- Executive summary
- Audit readiness checklist

## Assessment Assumptions

This is a fictional portfolio project. The organization, risks, findings, and control results are created for demonstration purposes only.

No real company data, confidential information, regulated data, or client information is included.
