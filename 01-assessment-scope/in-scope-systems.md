# In-Scope Systems

## Purpose

This document identifies the systems, applications, platforms, and processes included in the BayouTrust Digital Bank GRC assessment.

The purpose is to clearly define the boundaries of the assessment so control testing, evidence requests, risk identification, and remediation planning remain focused.

## In-Scope Environment Summary

The assessment includes systems and processes that support banking services, payment activity, customer data protection, identity and access management, security operations, vendor risk management, and audit readiness.

## In-Scope Systems

| System or Platform | Description | Criticality | Reason Included |
|---|---|---|---|
| Online Banking Platform | Customer-facing web application for account access and transactions. | High | Supports customer banking activity and sensitive data. |
| Mobile Banking Application | Mobile app used by customers to access banking services. | High | Supports customer access, authentication, and transactions. |
| Payment Processing Platform | Supports ACH, debit card, and payment-related activity. | High | Supports payment security and PCI readiness. |
| Identity Provider | Manages authentication, MFA, and single sign-on. | High | Controls employee and administrative access. |
| Customer Database | Stores customer account records and related information. | High | Contains sensitive customer and financial data. |
| Cloud Infrastructure | Hosts applications, APIs, databases, and services. | High | Supports critical business and technology operations. |
| SIEM Platform | Collects and monitors security logs. | High | Supports threat detection and incident response. |
| Vulnerability Scanner | Identifies vulnerabilities across systems. | High | Supports vulnerability management and risk reduction. |
| Backup Platform | Supports backup and recovery for critical systems. | High | Supports business continuity and recovery. |
| Ticketing System | Tracks incidents, service requests, and remediation activities. | Medium | Supports evidence of operational processes. |
| Vendor Management Platform | Tracks vendor onboarding, risk reviews, and vendor information. | Medium | Supports third-party risk management. |
| Policy Repository | Stores policies, standards, and governance documentation. | Medium | Supports audit readiness and governance. |

## Summary

The in-scope systems represent the major technology and business areas that support BayouTrust Digital Bank’s fictional banking, fintech, payment, and cloud operations.

These systems provide the foundation for the control assessment, evidence requests, risk register, gap analysis, POA&M, and executive reporting.
